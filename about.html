Faith Adventist Academy


Faith Adventist Academy is a private Christian school located in Taytay, Palawan, Philippines.
It was established in 2005 and affiliated with the Seventh-day Adventist Church.
The school offers a curriculum that integrates academic excellence with spiritual values,
aiming to develop well-rounded individuals who are prepared for both higher education and life.

PHILOSOPHY

The school believes in the holistic development of students,
focusing on their intellectual, physical, social, and spiritual growth.
It aims to create a nurturing environment that fosters a love for learning and a commitment to service.
